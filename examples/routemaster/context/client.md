## Product / Service

**What it does (one sentence):**
AI-powered route optimization software that reduces fuel costs and delivery times for last-mile delivery fleets.

**How it works (mechanism):**
RouteMaster ingests delivery orders, driver availability, vehicle constraints, and real-time traffic data. The RouteAI engine calculates optimal routes in under 90 seconds, factoring in time windows, vehicle capacity, and driver hours regulations. SmartDispatch then assigns routes to drivers and adjusts dynamically when conditions change mid-shift.

**Who it's for (buyer):**
Operations Managers and Fleet Managers at last-mile delivery companies with 20-500 vehicles. The Operations Manager typically evaluates and champions the tool. The CFO or COO approves the purchase based on cost savings data. Drivers and dispatchers are the daily users.

## UVP: Why Them

The only route optimizer built specifically for last-mile delivery fleets under 500 vehicles, delivering enterprise-grade AI optimization without the enterprise price tag or 6-month implementation.

## Proof: Case Studies with Numbers

1. **FreshBox** (grocery delivery, Berlin) - Reduced delivery costs by 40% in 3 months while handling 12,000 daily deliveries. Cut average route time from 6.2 hours to 4.3 hours per driver shift.

3. **GreenFleet** (EV fleet operator, Amsterdam) - Extended effective delivery range by 45% through smart charging stop integration. Reduced range anxiety complaints from drivers to near zero.
